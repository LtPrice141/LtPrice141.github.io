Lysol

Is a concentrated multi-purpose cleaner designed specifically for kitchen surfaces. It's formulated to effectively remove grease, grime, and food residue from various kitchen surfaces, including countertops, appliances, and sinks. Lysol Kitchen Pro is known for its powerful cleaning action and ability to kill 99.9% of bacteria. It's often used for daily kitchen cleaning and sanitizing to maintain a hygienic environment. Many consumers appreciate Ariel's ability to remove stubborn stains while maintaining the vibrancy of colors. It's often chosen for its ability to leave clothes smelling fresh and clean, even after multiple washes.

How To Use:
To Operate
Turn nozzle to desired position.

For Kitchen and Bathroom Cleaning
1. Spray dirty (soiled) area until thoroughly wet,
then wipe clean with clean cloth or sponge.
2. Repeat for stubborn stains.
3. No rinsing required.

For Disinfecting
1. Pre-clean surface.
2. Apply to surface until thoroughly wet.

For sanitizing
Leave for 30 seconds before wiping.

Caution
Keep the product out of reach of children and avoiding contact with eyes and skin;
if contact occurs, rinse thoroughly with water and seek medical attention if irritation persists.
It should not be ingested, and if swallowed, drink water and contact a doctor immediately.
Use in a well-ventilated area and avoid inhaling fumes.
Do not mix with other household chemicals, especially bleach, as this can release hazardous gases.
Store in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight, and always follow the specific safety instructions on the product packaging.
