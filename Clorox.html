Clorox

Disinfect kitchen surfaces, bathrooms, laundry, floors, and outdoor areas to eliminate mold, mildew, and germs, while removing stains from clothes, linens, floors, and patio furniture.

How To Use:
For Cleaning, Disinfecting and Deodorizing Hard, Nonporous Surfaces:
1. Pre-wash surface.
2. Mop or Wipe with disinfecting solution of 1 cup (250 ml) Clorox® per 5 L of water.
3. Allow solution to contact surface for at least 10 minutes.

For Laundry Use:
1. Add detergent.
2. Fill Clorox® to max line in dispenser or add 1 cup (250 ml) for top loading machines or 1/2 cup (125 ml) for front loading machines to wash water.
3.Add white clothes and start wash cycle.

Ingredients:
Water, Sodium Hypochlorite, Sodium Chloride, Sodium Chlorate, Poly(Diallyldimethylammonium Chloride), Sodium Hydroxide, Polyacrylic Acid, Sodium Bisulfite Terminated, Sodium Carbonate.

Caution
Use this product in well-ventilated areas to avoid fumes, wearing gloves and avoiding contact with skin and eyes, avoiding mixing with ammonia or other cleaners, following proper dilution instructions, and keeping it out of reach, especially when using concentrated forms, to prevent accidental ingestion.
