Lysol

Is intended to effectively eradicate germs and bacteria from diverse surfaces, resulting in a sanitary and fresh environment in your bathroom. Its strong composition removes difficult soap scum, mildew, and grime, leaving surfaces speaking clean and smelling great.

How To Use:
1. Flush toilet before cleaning
2.Apply 4 fl. oz. to coat the bowl including under the rim. Do not close lid.
To Clean:
Scrub entire bowl and under rim thoroughly.

To Disinfect:
Let sit for 15 minutes.

To Sanitize: Let sit for 30 seconds. Then flush.

4. Rinse brush in fresh water after use. Repeat if needed.

Ingredients:
Alkyl Dimethyl Benzyl Ammonium Chloride, Ethanol, Surfanctants, Fragrance, Colorants, Water.

Caution
Keep out of reach of children, can cause irritation to skin and eyes, harmful if injested, toxic inhalation risks, reacts with other chemicals, environmental hazard if not disposed properly. Ensure the container is sealed before disposing.
