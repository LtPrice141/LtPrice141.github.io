Mr. Muscle

Is a renowned cleaning brand noted for its potent solutions that successfully remove difficult stains and filth, especially in bathrooms. Its spray bottles are designed for easy application, making cleaning efficient while also leaving surfaces clean and delightfully fragrant.

How To Use:
For Regular Bathroom Cleaning
1. Mix Mr. Muscle Bathroom Cleaner with water (2:1) and pour on the surface.
2. Scrub and rinse with water. |

For Regular Toilet Cleaning
1. Pour Mr. Muscle Bathroom Cleaner directly on the stain.
2. Scrub and rinse with water.
3. Use regularly to keep your bathroom and toilet clean and smelling fresh.

For Disinfecting
1. After cleaning the entire bathroom with Mr. Muscle Bathroom Cleaner, mix 1 part of Mr. Muscle Bathroom Cleaner with 20 parts of water and apply the solution onto the surface.
2. Leave for 10 minutes then rinse with water.

Ingredients:
Surfactants, Sodium Hydroxide, Citric Acid, Fragrance, Preservatives, Water

Caution
Keep out of reach of children. May cause skin and eye irritation. Harmful if swallowed. Produces toxic fumes. Avoid contact with other chemicals.
