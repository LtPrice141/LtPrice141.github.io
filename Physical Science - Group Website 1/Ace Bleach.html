Ace Bleach

ACE Bleach is a well-known household cleaning product that contains sodium hypochlorite, a powerful disinfectant. It is used for whitening clothes, removing tough stains, and sanitizing various surfaces. With its strong germ-killing properties, ACE Bleach effectively eliminates bacteria, viruses, and fungi, making it a reliable choice for maintaining cleanliness and hygiene in homes.

How To Use:
For Use In a Washing Machine
1. Add your usual detergent as normal.
2. Pour ACE into the drawer marked with the CL symbol, up to the level guideline.

If your washing machine does not have a compartment with the CL symbol:
• If you do not intend to add any other products (e.g. softener),
you can pour your bleach into the ”other products” section.

• If however, you do intend to use other added products, start your pre-wash cycle,
and while the water is running pour in 1 1/2 cups of ACE (225ml).

3. Start the washing cycle immediately.
4. For best results, do not pour Ace into the detergent drawer or in the dosing ball with the detergent.

For Diluted Usage on Surface
150ml in 5 litres of water.

For Cleaning by Hand
1. Pour 1 glass of Ace into 10L of water.
2. Soak clothes for 20-30 minutes without adding detergent.
3. Rinse.
4. Wash as usual.

For Household Cleaning
Normal soil: Dilute 1 glass of Ace in 5L of water. Clean without rinsing.

Difficult Soil: Apply Ace pure directly on the surface. Rinse off.

Ingredients
5% Anionic Surfactants, Chlorine-Based Agents, Optical Brighteners, Perfumes.

Caution
The product is not suitable for wool, silk, and colored items. In the case of white items with resistant colors, try the product diluted on a hidden part of the fabric. The product is suitable for all nonporous surfaces: floors, tiles, bathtubs, showers, kitchen sinks. Do not use it on copper, brass, aluminum, and porous surfaces such as granite and wood. Do not expose to heat source or directly to sunlight. Keep it in the original bottle.
• Keep out of reach of children.
• Contains Sodium Hypochlorite bleach; may cause pink/purple/red spots if it contacts residues from other chemical products (e.g., creams).
• To remove stains, wash the item again with detergent and dry in direct sunlight.
• Do not mix ACE with detergent in the same washing ball or drawer.
• Always follow garment washing instructions.
