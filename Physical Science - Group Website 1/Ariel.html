Ariel

Is a leading laundry detergent brand renowned for its exceptional cleaning performance and long-lasting freshness. It's designed to tackle tough stains and leave clothes feeling soft and clean. With a variety of scents and formulas, Ariel offers a solution for different laundry needs, from everyday cleaning to deep stain removal. Many consumers appreciate Ariel's ability to remove stubborn stains while maintaining the vibrancy of colors. It's often chosen for its ability to leave clothes smelling fresh and clean, even after multiple washes.

How To Use:
1. Use the recommended amount of Ariel detergent powder based on the load size and soil level. Generally, for a regular load, 1-2 scoops should suffice. Refer to the packaging for specific measurements.
2. Add to the Washing Machine
Top-Loading Machine: Pour the detergent directly into the drum before adding clothes. If your machine has a detergent compartment, add the powder there instead.

Front-Loading Machine: Place the detergent in the designated compartment for powder.

3. Add your laundry to the washing machine, ensuring not to overload it for optimal cleaning.
5. Choose the appropriate wash cycle and temperature based on the fabric care labels of your clothes.
6. Begin the wash cycle.
7.Once the cycle is complete, remove your laundry and dry in the sun.

Ingredients:
Sodium Sulfate, Sodium C10-16 Alkylbenzenesulfonate, Sodium Carbonate, Sodium Silicate, Water, Bentonite, Sodium Polyacrylate, Zeolite, C10-16 Pareth, Fragrances, Cellulose Gum, Disodium Distyrylbiphenyl Disulfonate, Fluorescent Brightener 71, Subtilisin, Chromophore substituted polyoxyalkylene, Lipase, Pigment Red 5, Direct Violet 66, Pigment Green 7

Caution
Keep out of reach of children, can cause skin irritation, eye irritation, harmful if swallowed, and an environmental hazard if not disposed of properly. Make sure the container is empty and throw it in a trash can.
