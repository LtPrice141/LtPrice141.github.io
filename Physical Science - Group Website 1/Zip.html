Zip

Is a powerful cleaning product used for removing tough stains, grease, and grime from various surfaces. Ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, and cookware, it effectively cleans sinks, tiles, and stovetops, leaving them spotless. Its abrasive formula is designed to tackle stubborn dirt, making it a versatile solution for heavy-duty cleaning tasks.

How To Use:
1. Sprinkle the powder on a damp surface.
2. Scrub with a sponge or cleaning brush until the surface is clean.
3. Rinse thoroughly with water.

Ingredients:
Surfactants, Anionic, Non-ionic, Soap, Builders, Sodium Carbonate, Polycarboxylate, Citric Acid/Citrate, Hydrotropes & Solvents

Caution
Adequate ventilation is crucial to prevent fume inhalation. Wear gloves to avoid skin irritation. Do not mix bleach with other cleaners. Test surfaces in a small, discreet area to check for any adverse reactions. Once the container is empty, rinse it out and dispose of it in your recycling or waste bin, according to your local recycling program.
