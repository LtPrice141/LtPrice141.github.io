Zonrox

Zonrox is a concentrated bleach solution widely used in the Philippines for its powerful disinfectant properties. It's a popular household cleaning product, known for its ability to kill germs and bacteria on various surfaces. Zonrox is often used to sanitize floors, countertops, bathrooms, and laundry. It can also be diluted and used as a disinfectant for cleaning fruits and vegetables. However, it's important to follow the product's instructions carefully and use it in well-ventilated areas due to its strong odor and potential health risks if not handled properly.

How To Use:
1. Apply Zonrox directly on stains.
2. Rub gently
3. Wait for 5 minutes and wash as usual.

Ingredients:
Sodium Hypochlorite and Water

Caution
Can cause irritation, corrosion, toxic, and contains chlorine gas. Make sure the container is sealed before disposing.
