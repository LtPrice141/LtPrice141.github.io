Ajax

Ajax Multi-Purpose Cleaner is the effective and suitable cleaner for your home. With its proven anti-bacterial formula, it helps eliminate up to 99.9% bacteria, leaving your household surfaces clean and shiny. It also gives freshness that lasts for 24 hours* *Based on lab test with more concentrated usage Ajax Multi-Purpose Cleaner is suitable for cleaning floors and most household surfaces, such as marble, tiles, parquet, polished wood, terrazzo, cement, countertops, cupboard interiors, tabletops, toilet bowls and linoleum.

How To Use:
1. To use the cleaner, mix it with water in a bucket or spray bottle, depending on the surface being cleaned.
2. Apply the solution to the surface and scrub with a brush or sponge, then rinse with water.
3. For tougher stains, let the solution sit for a few minutes before scrubbing.

Ingredients:
Water, Sodium Dodecylbenzenesulfonate, Fragrance, C9-11 Pareth-8, Sodium Laureth Sulfate, Citric Acid, Glutaral, Colorants

Caution
Avoid using the cleaner on delicate surfaces like marble or brass. Store the cleaner in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep it out of reach of children and pets.
