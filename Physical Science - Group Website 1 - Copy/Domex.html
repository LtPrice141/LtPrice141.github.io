Domex

Is an excellent cleaning solution for removing thick grease and filth from a variety of surfaces, including kitchens and bathrooms. It cleans and disinfects, leaving a fresh aroma and providing a sanitary workplace.

How To Use:
1. Direct and squeeze nozzle under the toilet rim.
2. Wait for 20 mins, brush lightly and flush.

Ingredients:
Sodium Hyplochrite (Bleach), Surfactants, Fragrance, Water, pH adjusters, Thickening agents

Caution
Keep out of children. May cause skin and eye irritation. Harmful if swallowed. Produces toxic fumes. Avoid contact with other chemicals. Dispose of empty containers responsibly according to local regulations. Do not pour down the drain or into waterways.
