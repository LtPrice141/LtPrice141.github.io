Joy

Is a popular dishwashing detergent known for its effective grease-cutting power and pleasant scents. It's designed to clean dishes, utensils, and cookware thoroughly, leaving them sparkling clean and free from residue. Joy often comes in various fragrances, such as citrus, floral, or fruity, adding a refreshing touch to the dishwashing experience.

How To Use:
1. Add a few drops of Joy to a wet sponge.
2. Squeeze the sponge until it's sudsy.
3. Scrub the sponge onto dishes and utensils.
4. Rinse the dishes thoroughly.

Ingredients:
Anionic Surfactant, Builder, Anti-Redeposition Agents, Brightening Agents, Fragrance, Enzyme, Bleach

Caution
Keep out of reach of children, avoid contact with eyes (rinse with water if contact occurs),
do not ingest (drink water and seek medical attention if swallowed),
store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight, for external use only.
